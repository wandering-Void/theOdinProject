Veggie Omelette

Description

A veggie omelette is fluffy, colorful, and packed with healthy vegetables. It’s not only tasty but also quick to prepare for breakfast, lunch, or dinner. You can add your favorite veggies to make it just the way you like. It’s high in protein, so it keeps you energized throughout the day. This simple dish is both wholesome and satisfying.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1/4 cup chopped onions
  • 1/4 cup chopped tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup chopped capsicum
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1tbs oil

Steps

  1. Beat eggs with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat oil in a pan.
  3. Saute onions, tomatoes and capsicum.
  4. Pour in beaten eggs.
  5. Cook both sides until fluppy and golden.
