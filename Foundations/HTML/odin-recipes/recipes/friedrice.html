Fried Rice

Description

Fried rice is a fast and versatile dish that’s great for using up leftover rice. With a quick stir-fry of veggies and soy sauce, you get a filling meal in no time. It’s customizable—you can add eggs, chicken, or even paneer. The dish is a perfect balance of flavor and convenience. Plus, it all comes together in just one pan.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups coooked rice
  • 1 cup mixed veggies (carrots, beans, peas)
  • 2tbs soy sauce
  • 2tbs oil

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan.
  2. Stir-fry mixed vegetables until slightly soft.
  3. Add cooked rice to the pan.
  4. Pour in soy sauce and mix well.
  5. Stir fry for 2-3 minutes and serve.
