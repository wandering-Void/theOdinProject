Garlic Butter Pasta


Description

Garlic butter pasta is a simple and quick dish that brings out the rich flavor of garlic and butter. It’s light yet filling, making it perfect for days when you want something easy but tasty. Pair it with salad or bread to make it a full meal. You only need a handful of ingredients, so it’s budget-friendly. Best of all, it’s ready in under 15 minutes.

ingredients

  • 200g pasta
  • 3tbs butter
  • 3 garlic cloves (minced)
  • Salt and pepper
  • Parsley

Steps

  1. Cook pasta until al dente.
  2. Melt butter in a pan and saute garlic.
  3. Toss pasta in garlic butter.
  4. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Garnish with parsley and serve.
